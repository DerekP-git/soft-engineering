Historic Matches

Over the years, Morningside tennis has played some incredible matches. Here are a few memorable ones:

  • 2023 GPAC Championship: A dominant performance securing the conference title.
  • 2024 NAIA National Tournament: The team's first appearance at nationals in recent history.
  • 2021 NAIA Opening Round: A hard-fought battle that showcased the program's growth.
  • 2024 Women's GPAC Championship: Back-to-back titles with a perfect conference record
  • 2023 Women's National Tournament: First national tournament appearance in program history
