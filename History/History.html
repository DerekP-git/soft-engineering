History of Morningside Tennis

Founded: 1924.

Home Court: Mason Family Tennis Complex

Team Colors: Maroon and Black

Mascot: Mustang

1901: Tennis gets added to the Athletics Association - The Tennis Association is formed and is headed by President and Manager, Ross P. Brown.

1906: Ladies Tennis club Formed

1909: Tennis Courts Open

1921: New Tennis Court - The third court is to the west of the other courts and is ready for play.

1924: Women's Athletics Association is founded - Tennis gets added to the Women's Athletics Association in order to promote physical fitness among women.

1974: First year for Woman's Tennis - The first year of women's tennis at Morningside was short. The season ended with a 1-2 record. The women fought to two losses at Briar Cliff, but won their other game at Buena Vista.

1982: Women's Tennis Places 5th In Tourney - The Morningside women's tennis team completed their season with a 5th place in the Io-Kota Conference Tournament.

1984: Women's tennis team places second in IO-KOTA - The women's tennis team improved with the depth of new players to a 3-3 record for second place at the IO-KOTA Conference Tournament held at Westmar in October.
